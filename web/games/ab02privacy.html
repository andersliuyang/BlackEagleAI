Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the Android game Killer Apartment (the "Game"), developed by AndersLiu (QZQStudio). We value your privacy and are committed to maintaining a secure gaming environment.

2. Data Collection & Local-First Principle

Local Data Storage: In alignment with our commitment to privacy-focused technology and local-first architectures, all game progress—including acquired "Consciousness" energy, room keys (102-109), and mission states—is stored locally on your device.

Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). The Game does not require an account, email address, or phone number to play.

3. Permissions

The Game may request Storage Access solely to save and load your encrypted game files on your device. We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or location data.

4. Third-Party Services

The Game is designed to be a standalone experience. We do not utilize third-party advertising SDKs or external analytics tools that track user behavior across other apps or websites.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game contains themes of horror and suspense. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. Since all data is stored locally, parents can manage the Game's data directly via the device's application settings.
