How to Save LLM-Generated Content Directly to Notion

Send AI-generated content straight into your Notion to build a durable knowledge base.

Overview

Create a Notion integration, authorize a page, then set the token in BlackEagleAI to one-click save chats or articles to Notion.

1) Create a Notion integration and get the token

  1. Open the Notion integrations page: https://www.notion.so/my-integrations
  2. Click New integration, fill details (e.g., name: BlackEagleAI), and choose the workspace.
  3. Recommended permissions: Read content, Insert content, Update content.
  4. After creation, copy the Internal Integration Token (the Notion token).

2) Prepare the target page and authorize the integration

BlackEagleAI writes to the page you authorize, so you need:

  1. Create a page in Notion (recommend naming it BlackEagleAI).
  2. Open the page menu (···), choose Add connections, and connect the integration to this page.

3) Configure Notion inside BlackEagleAI

  1. Open BlackEagleAI Options in the browser.
  2. Go to More Settings → Notion, paste the token from step 1, and save.

4) One-click save articles to Notion

After setup, you can ask AI to draft, refine, or polish content, then:

  • Click Save to Notion in chat (or say “save this article to my Notion”).
  • The plugin writes to your authorized Notion page—no copy/paste needed.

5) Notes

  • Keep the Internal Integration Token safe; do not share it.
  • Ensure the integration has write access on the target page, otherwise saving will fail.

Summary

Linking BlackEagleAI with Notion keeps high-quality AI outputs as a long-term knowledge base, improving writing and curation efficiency.
